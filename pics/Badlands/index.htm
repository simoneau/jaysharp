These are pictures of the Pine Ridge Indian Reservation, South Dakota. I spent the first few years of my life growing up in the small town of Sharp's Corner. The house where I grew up was just behind the general store that you see in the last picture. The house was tore down many years ago, and the store has become a convience mart.
